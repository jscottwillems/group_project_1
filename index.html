    How to use this app

    CitySearch allows users to search for a city of their choice and receive relevant information about that city. The search results display photos, information such as ratings and reviews, and upcoming events in that city. about that city. City search is ideal for users who want to compare travel destinations or just find something fun to do in their local city.
